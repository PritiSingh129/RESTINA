About Us


Welcome to The Indian Thali, your destination for authentic Indian cuisine that tantalizes your taste buds and celebrates the rich culinary heritage of India. Located in the heart of the city, we pride ourselves on offering a memorable dining experience for food enthusiasts who crave the true essence of Indian flavors.


At The Indian Thali, we believe food is more than just sustenance; it’s a journey into the traditions, culture, and warmth of a community. Our menu is crafted to represent the diversity of India, featuring classic dishes from the northern regions to the southern coasts, along with specialties from the west and east. Every plate is a masterpiece, made with the freshest ingredients and a pinch of love.


Our skilled chefs bring years of experience and passion to the kitchen, ensuring each dish is cooked to perfection. From the sizzling tandoor grills to the aromatic curries, and from the crisp samosas to the delicate desserts, every bite tells a story of flavor and tradition.


We are committed to creating an inviting atmosphere where families and friends can come together to share delicious meals and create cherished memories. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion, hosting a corporate lunch, or simply enjoying a casual dinner, we aim to make every visit extraordinary.


We also offer catering services for events and gatherings, ensuring your guests get a taste of India's finest delicacies.


Come join us at The Indian Thali and embark on a flavorful journey that will leave you coming back for more!



Our Menu


At The Indian Thali, our menu is a culinary journey across India, featuring signature dishes from every region. Whether you’re craving bold spices, creamy curries, or sweet treats, we’ve got something for everyone!

Starters

Samosa

Golden-fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas.

Paneer Tikka

Chunks of marinated cottage cheese grilled to perfection.

Chicken Tandoori

Juicy chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in a tandoor.

Aloo Tikki

Spiced potato patties served with tangy tamarind and mint chutney.


Main Courses

Butter Chicken

Classic chicken curry with a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce.

Dal Makhani

Slow-cooked black lentils in a buttery, creamy gravy.

Rogan Josh

Aromatic lamb curry cooked with Kashmiri spices.

Vegetable Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables and spices.


Breads and Slices

Garlic Naan

Soft, buttery flatbread infused with garlic.

Whole Wheat Roti

Traditional Indian unleavened bread.

Crispy Papad

Thin, crispy lentil wafers served with chutneys.


Desserts

Gulab Jamun

Soft milk dumplings soaked in rose-flavored syrup.

Rasmalai

Delicate paneer balls in sweet, creamy milk flavored with cardamom.

Kheer

Traditional Indian rice pudding garnished with nuts.

Jalebi

Deep-fried spirals soaked in sugar syrup, crispy and sweet.


Beverages

Mango Lassi

A refreshing blend of yogurt, mango, and a touch of sugar.

Masala Chai

Traditional Indian spiced tea with milk.

South Indian Filter Coffee

Rich, aromatic coffee served with milk and sugar.

Fresh Coconut Water

Hydrating and refreshing, straight from the coconut.
